APPLICATION OF PANTERRA NETWORKS, INC.
FOR AUTHORIZATION TO OBTAIN NUMBERING RESOURCES
By this Application, PanTerra Networks, Inc. (“PanTerra” or “Company”), an
interconnected Voice over Internet Protocol (“VoIP”) provider, seeks to obtain direct access to
numbering resources from the North American Numbering Plan Administrator and the Pooling
Administrator (“Numbering Administrators”) pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3)(i) of the
Commission’s Rules.1
PanTerra provides the following information in support of this Application:

B. § 52.15(g)(3)(i)(B)
PanTerra hereby acknowledges that authorization to obtain numbering resources under
Section 52.15(g) of the Commission’s Rules is subject to compliance with applicable Commission
numbering rules, as well as to the numbering authority delegated to the states. The Company
hereby also acknowledges that this authorization is subject to compliance with industry guidelines
and practices regarding numbering, as applicable to telecommunications carriers. The numbering
resources that are the subject of this Application will be used to provide interconnected VoIP
services initially in California, and ultimately nationwide. Accordingly, to the extent required,
PanTerra requests the Commission grant it authority to obtain numbering resources in all states.
C. § 52.15(g)(3)(i)(C)
PanTerra acknowledges that it must file requests for numbers with the relevant state
commission(s) at least 30 days before requesting numbers from the Numbering Administrators.

Application. PanTerera respectfully requests that these agreements be accorded confidential
treatment pursuant to Section 0.459 of the Commission’s Rules.2 As Exhibit B, PanTerra provides

Exhibit C
Managerial Information
PanTerra’s key management and technical personnel are listed below. None of the
identified personnel are being or have been investigated by the Commission or any law
enforcement or regulatory agency for failure to comply with any law, rule, or order.
Arthur Chang, President and CEO, Board Member
Arthur Chang brings over 20 years of hi-tech executive experience to PanTerra Networks. Artie,
a Silicon Valley serial entrepreneur, held senior executive positions at several successful
companies in Silicon Valley. Prior to PanTerra Networks, Artie was the CEO of Cradle
Technologies, a multi-core multimedia DSP company and SoloPoint Communications, a
provider of telecom equipment. Artie started his career at Bell Laboratories.
Jeff Boucher, Chief Financial Officer & Vice President of Finance
Jeff Boucher brings over 20 years of finance, accounting and operations experience in SaaS
technology companies. Prior to PanTerra Networks, Jeff was the VP of Finance at Power Assure,
a SaaS provider of data center energy management software. Prior to that he was the VP of
Finance at Bytemobile, a company acquired by Citrix Systems. Jeff started his career at the
public accounting firm Frank, Rimerman & Co., where he earned his California CPA license as
an audit manager.
Robert Perez Mainor, Executive Vice President, Operations
Robert Mainor has over 20 years of senior level executive experience in the communications
industry. Rob has held strategic roles in marketing, sales, business development and operations
and is considered a key pioneer in bringing VoIP to the masses. Prior to PanTerra Networks, Rob
was the co-founder and COO of SunRocket. Prior to SunRocket, Rob was the President of
Xpedite Systems, a $200M multi-modal communications services company. At COLT Telecom
in London, he built their pan-European business as president and Managing Director. He
founded and sold Seara Information Strategy Corp to CompuServe, where he served as VP of
Product Management and Marketing.

David Ryan, Vice President of Sales
David Ryan brings with him over 20 years of sales leadership to PanTerra Networks. Dave has
held strategic roles in sales leadership, sales, operations and business development in the UCaaS,
SaaS and telecommunications industries. Prior to joining PanTerra Networks, Dave was
Regional Director of Sales at Nextel Partners, Inc. a wireless communication provider. Prior to
Nextel Partners, Mr. Ryan was the Regional Director of Sales at Verizon Wireless.
Ranga Rao Vutukuru, Vice President of Development
Ranga Rao Vutukuru has over 20 years of experience in software engineering, management and
strategic direction setting. Prior to PanTerra Networks, Ranga was the Chief Architect at
eMatrix. Prior to eMatrix, Ranga held engineering architect positions at MedPlexus, Ericsson,
and NHCL.
Jerome Friesenhahn, Vice President of Customer Success
Jerome Friesenhahn has over 20 years of hi-tech experience with a focus on cloud-based unified
communications since 2000. Prior to PanTerra Networks, Jerome was EVP with Allnet Services,
where he managed all sales and technical operations, including building strong business
relationships with early adopters of cloud unified communications providers.
Dave Immethun, Senior Director of Marketing
Dave Immethun has spent over 20 years in various marketing and sales positions in the
communications field. Prior to PanTerra Networks, Dave was the Sr. Director of Sales and
Director of Product Marketing at Packet8/8x8 Inc. Prior to 8x8 Inc., Dave was Director of
Product Management at AltiGen Communications, an IP-PBX equipment provider. Mr.
Immethun started his career at Nortel Networks.

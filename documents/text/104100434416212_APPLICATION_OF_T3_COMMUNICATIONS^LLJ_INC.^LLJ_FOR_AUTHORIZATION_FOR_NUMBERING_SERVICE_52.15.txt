APPLICATION OF T3 COMMUNICATIONS, INC., FOR AUTHORIZATION
TO OBTAIN NUMBERING RESOURCES
T3 Communications, Inc., (“T3”), pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3)(i) of the Commission’s Rules,
respectfully requests authorization to obtain numbering as described below. Under the
Commission's Numbering Order,1 an interconnected VoIP provider may obtain numbering
resources from the Numbering Administrator upon a showing that it is authorized to provide
service in the area for which the numbering resources are requested. Such authorization may be
obtained upon an application to the Commission containing the information detailed in Section
52.15 (g)(3)(i)(A) to (G) of the Commission’s Rules. T3 hereby requests the Commission grant it
that authorization. In support of this application, T3 provides the following information in support
of this application:

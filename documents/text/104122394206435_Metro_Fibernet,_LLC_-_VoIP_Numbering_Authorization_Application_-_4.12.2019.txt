APPLICATION OF METRO FIBERNET, LLC
FOR AUTHORIZATION TO OBTAIN NUMBERING RESOURCES
Metro Fibernet, LLC (“MFN”) files this application with the Federal Communications
Commission (the “Commission”) for authorization to acquire telephone numbers directly from the
North American Numbering Plan Administrator and the Pooling Administrator (the “Numbering
Administrators”) pursuant to Commission rule section 52.15(g)(3).1
As set forth in the Commission’s Numbering Order,2 an interconnected VoIP provider may
obtain numbering resources from the Numbering Administrator upon showing that it is authorized
to provide service in the area for which the numbering resources are requested. Such authorization
may be obtained upon an application to the Commission containing the information in Sections
52.15(g)(3)(i)(A)-(F) of the Commission’s Rules. MFN hereby requests the Commission grant it
the authorization.
MFN provides the following information in support of its application:

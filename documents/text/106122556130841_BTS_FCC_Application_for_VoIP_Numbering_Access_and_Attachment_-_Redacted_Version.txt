Background on BTS
BTS began providing telecommunications services in 1993, initially offering a variety of
wholesale and retail long distance services. In 1996, BTS established direct routes for the
termination of international voice services throughout Latin America and the Caribbean. In
2001, BTS expanded its reach with strategic agreements with foreign operators in Latin America
and Africa. Today, BTS has offices in Miami, Florida, Zaragosa, Spain, Singapore and Dubai.
BTS has interconnection agreements with over 180 carriers worldwide and carries traffic to and
from 200 countries.
BTS is a member of numerous industry organizations, including the i3 Forum, GBET
Association, GW Tracker and ITW’s Global Leadership Forum’s Communications Blockchain
Network.

Re: Notice of Change in Ownership of Quality Voice & Data, Inc.
Dear Ms. Dortch:
By this letter, Quality Voice & Data Inc. (“QVD” or “Company”) hereby notifies the
Commission of a change in ownership of QVD, pursuant to 47 C.F.R § 52.15(g)(3)(x). The
Company was granted authority to obtain access to numbering resources on April 6, 2020 in WC
Docket No. 20-42.1
Under the terms of an agreement between the parties executed on November 26, 2024
(“the Transaction”), Dean and Angela Garfinkel, the sole shareholders of QVD, transferred their
shares to Joseph Scarpelli and Guy Scarpelli, who will continue to operate the Company under
the QVD name.
The Company’s underlying providers will continue to provide termination services
required to ensure that QVD will continue to provide services and can to any location for which
it requests numbers within 60 days, pursuant to 47 C.F.R. § 52.15(g)(3)(ii)(H). The Transaction
will not result in a change in QVD’s Operating Carrier Number (“OCN”).
In support of this change, the parties provide the following updated information for the
applicable required sections of 47 C.F.R. § 52.15(g)(3)(ii) for the new ownership of the
Company as a result of the Transaction:

Biographical Information
Joseph Scarpelli - Joe Scarpelli is the President of QVD and holds a degree in Mechanical
Engineering. He started his professional career designing fossil power plants and environmental
retrofits before joining the family business. Mr. Scarpelli brings over ten years of expertise to
QVD in call center operations and related endeavors. Mr. Scarpelli also has a unique passion
and expertise for the technical side of the telecommunication industry.
Guy Scarpelli - Guy Scarpelli is a Director of QVD. Mr. Scarpelli is a veteran in the Call Center
Industry building relationships for over 37 years. Mr. Scarpelli served on the board of the Direct
Marketing Associations Telephone Marketing Conference and the American Telemarketing
Associations Chicago Chapter board for several years and has a robust resume of clients he has
served in the call center industry.

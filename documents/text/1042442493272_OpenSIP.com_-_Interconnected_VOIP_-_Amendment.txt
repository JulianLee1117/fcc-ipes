Pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3)(i) of the Commission’s Rules, OpenSIP.com, LLC
hereby wishes to submit an amendment to its application requesting authorization
to obtain numbering resources.
1. OpenSip.com LLC confirms that there is no foreign ownership of
OpenSIP.com LLC either by individual or entity.

3. We would like amend 47 CFR § 64.604(5)(iii) to 47 CFR § 64.604(c)(5)(iii)

application.
4. In the original application under section 52.15(g)(3)(i)(D), it was stated that
"As proof of its facilities readiness, OpenSIP.com has attached to this
application (1) An agreement between OpenSIP.com and its carrier partner
providing that the carrier partner will host OpenSIP.com numbers on its
switches and provide PSTN connectivity for inbound calls to OpenSIP.com
numbers..." OpenSIP.com LLC wishes to amend and correct this sense to "As
proof of its facilities readiness, OpenSIP.com has attached to this application
(1) An agreement between OpenSIP.com and its carrier partner providing
that the carrier partner will host OpenSIP.com numbers on its switches and

provide PSTN connectivity for both inbound and outbound traffic to and from
OpenSIP.com telephone numbers."

APPLICATION OF WIRETAP TELECOM, LLC FOR AUTHORIZATION TO OBTAIN
NUMBERING RESOURCES
Wiretap Telecom, LLC (“WTT”), pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3)(i) of the Federal
Communications Commission’s (“Commission’s”) Rules, respectfully requests authorization to
obtain numbering resources as described below.
As set forth in the Commission’s Numbering Order1, an interconnected VoIP provider
may obtain numbering resources from the Numbering Administrator upon a showing that it is
authorized to provide service in the area for which the numbering resources are requested. Such
authorization may be obtained upon an application to the Commission containing the information
detailed in Sections 52.15(g)(3)(i)(A)-(F) of the Commission’s Rules. WTT hereby requests the
Commission grant it that authorization. In support of this application, WTT provides the
following information:

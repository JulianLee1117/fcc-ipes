AMENDMENT TO APPLICATION OF AHOI LLC
FOR AUTHORIZATION TO OBTAIN NUMBERING RESOURCES
Ahoi LLC, pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3) of the Commission’s Rules, respectfully
requests authorization to obtain numbering as described below. This Amendment to the
Application incorporates and replaces the original Application and Supplemental Filing, and
further supplements those prior filings. This Amendment does not replace the previously-filed
Exhibits.
Under the Commission's Numbering Order,1 an interconnected VoIP provider may obtain
numbering resources from the Numbering Administrator upon a showing that it is authorized to
provide service in the area for which the numbering resources are requested. Such authorization
may be obtained upon an application to the Commission containing the information detailed in
Section 52.15 (g)(3) of the Commission’s Rules. Ahoi hereby requests the Commission grant it
that authorization. In support of this application, Ahoi provides the following information:

§ 52.15(g)(3)(ii)(B)
Ahoi hereby acknowledges that authorization to obtain numbering resources under Section
52.15(g) of the Commission's Rules is subject to compliance with applicable Commission

§ 52.15(g)(3)(ii)(C)
Ahoi is committed to providing full support for its customers concerning Robocall

ActionVox, Inc. (“ActionVox”), pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3)(i) of the
Commission’s Rules, respectfully requests authorization to obtain numbering resources as
described below.
1
As set forth in the Commission’s Numbering Order , an interconnected VoIP
provider may obtain numbering resources from the Numbering Administrator upon a
showing that it is authorized to provide service in the area for which the numbering
resources are requested. Such authorization may be obtained upon an application to
the Commission containing the information detailed in Sections 52.15(g)(3)(i)(A)-(G) of the
Commission’s Rules. ActionVox hereby requests the Commission grant it that
authorization. In support of this application, ActionVox provides the following
information:

Management Biographies

Toufic Mobarak
Toufic Mobarak is the founder and President of MobileSphere, and a longstanding business leader
in the software and telecommunications industry.
Since its founding in 2003, the company has pioneered popular services slydial, slybroadcast, and
slyText for its millions of users, and thousands of global enterprises, nonprofit organizations, and
universities. In 2016, MobileSphere was granted a patent for its straight-to-voicemail technology.
Previously, Toufic was a co-founder and president of ConeXus and held international leadership
positions at GTE in Strategy and Corporate Planning.
Toufic received his Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in International Economics from Sorbonne in
Paris, France, and his master’s degree in business administration from Thunderbird’s American School of
International Management in Arizona.

Ashou Han
Ashou Han is the MobileSphere’s Chief Technology Officer and has worked with the company
for over 10 years. He is responsible for ensuring that MobileSphere’s technologies are in line with their
strategy, used efficiently and securely. He is also responsible for evaluating and implementing new
systems and infrastructures.
Ashou studied at the University of Maine and received his Bachelor’s degree in Computer
Science.
Beth George
Beth George is the Director of Marketing and Operations for MobileSphere and has worked with
the company for over 8 years. Beth oversees all of MobileSphere’s marketing initiatives and customer
relations and assist in product development.
Beth attended Boston College's Carroll School of Management and later achieved her MBA from
the University of Massachusetts-Boston.

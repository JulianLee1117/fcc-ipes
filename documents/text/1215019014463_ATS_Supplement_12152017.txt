SUPPLEMENT OF ADVANCED TELECOM SOLUTIONS, LLC
Pursuant to a request for additional information from Federal Communications
Commission (“Commission”) Staff, Advanced Telecom Solutions, LLC (“ATS”) hereby
provides the following additional information in support of its Application for Authorization to
Obtain Number Resources.
1. ATS is a provider of “Interconnected VoIP Service” as that term is defined in the
Commission’s rules.
2. ATS provides a copy of its contract with Inteliquent under confidential seal.
3. The direct telephone number and email for Roman Shpount are as follows: (202) 262-
8672 and rshpount@turbobridge.com.
4. ATS is 100% U.S. owned, and has zero foreign ownership.
5. ATS plans to provide interconnected VoIP service to customers located in the United
States, and the telephone numbers ATS will seek to obtain will be used for U.S.-based
services. ATS does not intend to assign U.S.-based telephone numbers to customers
located in foreign countries.
In addition to the five items specifically requested by Commission Staff, ATS attaches to this
Supplement ATS’s Articles of Organization and Certificate of Good Standing from the State of
Maryland Department of Assessments and Taxation.

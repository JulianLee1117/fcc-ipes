APPLICATION - OF BARR TELL USA, INC.
FOR AUTHORIZATION TO OBTAIN NUMBERING RESOURCES
BARR TELL USA, INC. (“BARR TELL”) pursuant to Section 52.15(g)(3)(i) of the Commission’s Rules,
respectfully requests authorization to obtain numbering as described below.
Under the Commission’s Numbering Order,1 an interconnected VoIP provider may obtain numbering
resources from the Numbering Administrator upon a showing that it is authorized to provide service in the area for
which the numbering resources are requested. Such authorization may be obtained upon an application to the
Commission containing the information detailed in Section 52.15.(g)(3)(i)(A)-(G) of the Commission’s Rules.
BARR TELL hereby requests the Commission grant it that authorization. In support of this application, BARR
TELL provides the following information:

D. § 52.15(g)(3)(i)(D)
BARR TELL hereby sets forth its capability to provide service within 60 days of the numbering resources activation
date. To demonstrate its facilities readiness, Barr Tell USA, Inc., currently retains many Telephone numbering
resources from multiple Rate centers in a handful of States where it has been Licensed as a Facilities Based CLEC.
It has been providing telephone services to the Public for over 15 years. As such, Barr Tell operates daily with a
fully integrated back office support team with the ability to schedule and process LNP orders from customers as well
as an experienced provisioning department capable of handling bulk numbering ports. In addition, BARR TELL has
an agreement in place with a Facilities Based CLEC partner to route traffic to the ILECs. This also includes providing
E 911 services and has a NENA ID of BAA1
In addition, BARR TELL has attached to this application, Exhibits A & B. Exhibit A is an Agreement between
BARR TELL and its carrier partner, who will host BARR TELL’s numbering resources on its TDM switching
platform and provide connectivity to the PSTN for inbound calls to the new BARR TELL’s telephone numbers in

the States where Barr Tell is not a bonafide CLEC. Exhibit B is a copy of an interconnection agreement between
one of BARR TELL's CLEC’s carrier partners and an ILEC in the State of New Hampshire.
With this VOIP Authorization it would provide the flexibility to use those newly acquired telephone numbers for its
VoIP business customers within sixty (60) days of authorization and either return or port service numbers
previously acquired from other sources
